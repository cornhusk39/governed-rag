Item 2. Management’s Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations

The following discussion should be read in conjunction with the condensed consolidated financial statements and the related notes included elsewhere in this report. This section contains forward-looking statements that involve risks and uncertainties.

Net sales for the quarter rose 22% year over year, reflecting broad strength across the industrial automation segment. Services revenue grew 31% as the installed base expanded and attach rates improved. Gross margin expanded by approximately 120 basis points on favorable product mix and manufacturing efficiencies realized at the new Ohio facility.

Operating expenses increased in absolute terms but declined as a percentage of net sales, as the Company continued to invest in research and development while maintaining disciplined control over general and administrative costs. Research and development spending was concentrated in autonomous navigation and machine-vision capabilities expected to reach general availability in the next fiscal year.

Liquidity remained strong, with cash and cash equivalents and short-term investments providing ample coverage of near-term obligations. The Company expects existing balances and cash generated from operations to be sufficient to satisfy working-capital needs, planned capital expenditures, and contractual commitments for at least the next twelve months.
